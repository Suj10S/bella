About Us

Bella Cafe is a cozy haven in the heart of Damak, offering a perfect blend of delicious food, aromatic coffee, and a warm ambiance. Whether you're looking for a quick bite or a relaxing meal with friends and family, we bring you the best of local and international flavors.

Visit us at Traffic Chowk to experience the true taste of Jhapa. Our doors are open from 7:30 AM to 9:30 PM, ready to serve you with a smile.
